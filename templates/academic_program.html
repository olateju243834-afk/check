Undergraduate Academic Programs

Comprehensive B.Eng Agricultural and Environmental Engineering Program designed to produce competent engineers for sustainable agricultural development.

5-Year Program COREN Accredited Industry Recognized

B.Eng Agricultural and Environmental Engineering

A comprehensive 5-year undergraduate program that integrates engineering principles with agricultural and environmental sciences to address challenges in food production, environmental sustainability, and rural development.

Duration

5 Years (10 Semesters)
Plus 6 months SIWES

Class Size

Small class sizes for
personalized attention

Accreditation

Fully accredited by
COREN and NUC

Graduation Requirements

Academic Requirements
  • Minimum of 180 credit units
  • Minimum CGPA of 1.50
  • Successful completion of SIWES
  • Satisfactory final year project
  • No outstanding fees
Professional Development
  • Eligible for COREN registration
  • Industry internship experience
  • Research presentation skills
  • Technical report writing
  • Teamwork and leadership

Career Prospects

Our graduates pursue diverse career paths in various sectors
