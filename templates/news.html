Research January 15, 2025

International Symposium on Sustainable Agriculture Engineering

Our department successfully hosted the International Symposium on Sustainable Agriculture Engineering, bringing together researchers, industry experts, and students from across West Africa. The event featured groundbreaking research presentations on precision agriculture, renewable energy in farming, and climate-smart agricultural technologies.

Key highlights included the unveiling of our new IoT-based irrigation monitoring system and partnerships with leading agricultural technology companies for student internships and research collaborations.
